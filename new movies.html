Retro

4.2/5

Retro" is a Tamil action-drama-love story that follows Paari, a gangster's adopted son, who seeks peace after falling in love with Rukmini. While the film boasts a strong performance from Suriya and visually impressive scenes, it struggles with a cluttered narrative and an uneven second half, ultimately leaving many viewers wanting more.


Lucky Baskar

4.8/5

Lucky Baskhar" is a film about a man named Baskhar who, after enduring financial hardship, embarks on a path of deception and exploitation to improve his life. The movie explores themes of ambition, family, and the corrupting influence of wealth, and it is a well-acted and engaging film that raises questions about morality and societal inequality.
