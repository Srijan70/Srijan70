Best of all time

Breaking Bad

4.9/5

"Breaking Bad" is a highly acclaimed and critically praised crime drama that follows the transformation of Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher, into a drug lord as he battles terminal cancer and seeks to secure his family's financial future. The show is known for its complex characters, compelling storytelling, and realistic dialogue. It explores themes of morality, power, greed, and the consequences of choices.


Money Heist

4.9/5

"Money Heist" is a Spanish crime drama series that gained international acclaim, particularly on Netflix. Reviews generally highlight its thrilling plot, well-developed characters, and unique blend of crime and drama. While the early seasons are widely praised for their captivating narrative and suspense, later seasons have received mixed feedback, with some critics noting plot inconsistencies and a decline in coherence.
